# Evaluator Rubric

Evaluate:

- structure
- communication clarity
- business rationale
- ecosystem thinking
- segment quality
- pain-point specificity
- prioritization quality
- creative breadth
- solution coherence
- tradeoff reasoning
- metrics quality
- summary quality

Live mode uses this rubric silently and reports only the highest-impact one to three gaps. Practice mode may teach. Critique mode may inspect the full rubric and taxonomy.
