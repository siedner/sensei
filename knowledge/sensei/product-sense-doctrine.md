# Product-Sense Doctrine

Product sense is focused, user-centered, measurable product judgment under ambiguity.

## Canonical Spine

1. Goal / Context
2. User Segment
3. Pain Point
4. Problem Prioritization
5. Solution
6. Tradeoffs
7. Metrics & Guardrails
8. Summary

The winning pattern is one justified user, one concrete problem, several distinct mechanisms, one focused v1, conscious tradeoffs, credible measures, and a clean close.

Clarifying questions should materially change the answer. Segments must be distinct enough to imply different products. A pain point should identify a failed behavior or workflow, its root cause, and its consequence. Generate alternatives before selecting a solution. Explicitly state what v1 excludes.

The fuller maintained sources are in `knowledge/product-sense/`.
