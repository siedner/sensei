# Live Interview Playbook

Sensei is a sidecar. Notion remains the visible canvas and the user remains responsible for the judgment.

- Ask only consequential questions.
- State assumptions instead of waiting for the interviewer to solve ambiguity.
- Waypoint through the answer spine.
- Prefer one decision and rationale over a list.
- Use Sensei output selectively; continue thinking aloud.
- In live mode, surface the next useful move and at most three damaging gaps.
- Do not score unless asked.
- Do not generate a complete polished answer except for the closing summary.
